Facts about cat

  • Cats are the most popular pet in the USA. There are 88 million cats kept as pets!
  • Cats have over 30 muscles that control their ears.
  • Cats sleep 70% of their lives, and in some pretty crazy positions.
  • A cat fact you've probably noticed in your feline, your kitty has the ability to rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  • A cat’s nose is as unique as a human fingerprint. One cat, Oz, even has a Map of Australia on Her Nose!
  • Unlike humans, cats cannot detect sweetness – which explains why they are not drawn to it.
